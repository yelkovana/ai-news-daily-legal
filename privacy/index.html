Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

This policy describes how AI News Daily (the “Service”) handles information when you use it to publish content to third-party platforms such as TikTok and YouTube. AI News Daily is operated by Atakan Yelkovan.

1. What we collect

  • OAuth tokens issued by platforms you connect (e.g. TikTok, YouTube). These are used only to publish content on your behalf within the scopes you approve.
  • Basic account identifiers returned by those platforms (e.g. account username or open id) so we can show which account is connected.
  • Content you generate through the Service: script text, rendered video files, thumbnails, and associated metadata you provide.
  • Operational logs (timestamps, error codes, API call outcomes) for debugging and reliability.

We do not collect your platform passwords. We do not read your inbox, direct messages, private videos, followers list, or analytics beyond what is strictly required to publish content.

2. How we use information

  • To authenticate with connected platforms and publish content you instruct us to publish.
  • To display the status of upload/publish jobs.
  • To maintain and debug the Service.

We do not sell your information. We do not use it for advertising or profiling. We do not share it with third parties except the specific platform to which you asked us to publish.

3. Storage and security

Tokens and generated content are stored on the operator’s own machines and standard cloud infrastructure, protected by operating-system permissions and, where applicable, provider-level encryption at rest and in transit (HTTPS). Access is limited to the operator.

4. Retention

OAuth tokens are kept until you revoke access or the platform expires them. Rendered videos and logs are kept for as long as they are operationally useful and deleted on request.

5. Your rights

  • Revoke access at any time from the connected platform’s settings (e.g. TikTok → Settings → Security → Manage app permissions; Google Account → Security → Third-party access).
  • Request deletion of any data we hold about you by emailing the contact below. We will delete it within 30 days.
  • Access or correction requests can be sent to the same contact.

6. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Third-party platforms

When you connect TikTok, YouTube, or other services, their own privacy policies govern how they handle your data. See:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Google / YouTube Privacy Policy

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
